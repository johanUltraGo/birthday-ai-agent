# 🎨 Feature Specifications

## 1. Student Data Management
*   **Data Entry:** Form-based entry for student name, class, and date of birth.
*   **Photo Uploads:** Integration with local storage for student portraits to be used in posters.
*   **Registry View:** Searchable/sortable list of all registered students.

## 2. Smart Birthday Detection
*   **Daily Alerts:** Automatic identification of students celebrating birthdays today.
*   **Upcoming Queue:** A 7-day lookahead to help the media team prepare posters in advance.
*   **Leap Year Support:** Robust handling for February 29th birthdays.

## 3. AI-Powered Content Generation
*   **Gemini Integration:** Uses `gemini-1.5-flash` to generate personalized, school-appropriate birthday wishes.
*   **Context Awareness:** Quotes focus on "growth," "future," and "excellence" as per school standards.

## 4. Automated Poster Studio
*   **Dynamic Compositing:** Merges student photos with AI quotes and branding elements.
*   **Themed Layouts:** Professional blue/white school theme with decorative borders.
*   **Export Formats:** High-resolution PNG output suitable for digital sharing or printing.

## 5. Reporting & Communication
*   **Monthly Reports:** Aggregated views of all birthdays for a specific month.
*   **WhatsApp Integration:** One-click sharing bridge to send posters to school social groups.
*   **Activity Logs:** Real-time tracking of poster generation and system notifications.
