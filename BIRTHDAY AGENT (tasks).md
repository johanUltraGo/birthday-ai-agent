# 🎂 Birthday Reminder AI Agent

## Task‑1: Student Data Collection & Storage

Step 1: Create a student data entry form (Name, Class, Birthday)  
Step 2: Validate the entered data (no empty fields, correct date format)  
Step 3: Store verified data in the database ([birthday details for students](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1mVwoFAnLyHi-3Z5JqeEGw9je6Wvr367d/edit?usp=drive_link&ouid=102563065876709377588&rtpof=true&sd=true) )  
Step 4: Confirm successful data storage

## Task‑2: Birthday Detection Logic

Step 1: Retrieve today’s date from the system  
Step 2: Fetch all student birthday records from the database  
Step 3: Compare the current month & day with the stored birthdays  
Step 4: Identify today’s and upcoming birthdays  
Step 5: Prepare a list of detected birthdays

## Task‑3: Notification System

Step 1: Generate a birthday alert message automatically  
Step 2: Send notification to teacher/dashboard  
Step 3: Log notification status  
Step 4: Confirm successful delivery

## Task‑4: Monthly Birthday Report Generation

Step 1: Filter students by selected month  
Step 2: Generate a formatted monthly birthday list  
Step 3: Export report (document)  
Step 4: Share the report with the school office

Task-5: AI Poster Template Generation & Media Sharing  
Step 1: Collect birthday student details (Name, Class, Date of Birth)  
Step 2: Generate a personalised birthday message using AI  
Step 3: Create a formatted birthday poster template  
Step 4: Review and finalise the generated content  
Step 5: Send the final poster template to the school media team for publication
